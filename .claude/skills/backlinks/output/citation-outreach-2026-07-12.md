# Workflow 3 — LLM Citation Gap — action plan — 2026-07-12

**Goal:** get MouDevPro onto the pages LLMs cite when a Moroccan buyer asks "meilleure agence / meilleur développeur web au Maroc". MouDevPro is currently absent from every page found.

**Key finding:** the "top agencies" *listicles* are mostly written by agencies that rank themselves #1 (Twice Box, Rythmedia, developpeur-informatique.ma) or are pay-to-play (Listaaj). Low odds and/or paid. **The real win is the neutral directories LLMs actually cite — free self-registration.**

---

## Priority 1 — Free directory profiles (do these first, no pitch needed)

These are self-service. Register once, and you enter the exact pages ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini quote.

- [ ] **Clutch** → https://clutch.co (Get Listed). The most-cited of all. Weighs **verified client reviews** heavily — line up 1–2 real clients to leave a review right after you create the profile.
- [ ] **Sortlist** → https://www.sortlist.com (rejoindre en tant qu'agence). Free profile; select categories *web development* + *e-commerce*, cities Casablanca/Rabat/Marrakech.
- [ ] **TechBehemoths** → https://techbehemoths.com (Add your company). Free, quick, well-indexed.
- [ ] **GoodFirms** → https://www.goodfirms.co (Get Listed). Free; fill services + portfolio.
- [ ] **The Manifest** → no direct signup; it's populated from **Clutch**, so getting on Clutch auto-lists you here.

**Profile content to reuse everywhere:**
- Positioning: *Développeur web freelance — React/Next.js, WordPress, WooCommerce. Casablanca, Rabat, Marrakech. Livraison 7–10 jours, paiement après livraison.*
- Portfolio: your live projects + the stats page (moudevpro.com/blog/statistiques-web-maroc-2026) as a credibility asset.
- Same NAP (name/URL/phone) across all → consistency helps both SEO and LLM confidence.

> ⚠️ Honest caveat: some directories frame entries as "companies/agencies". You can list as an independent studio — but a few (Clutch especially) rank largely on **verified reviews**, so without 1–2 reviews you'll be listed but ranked low. The review step is what actually moves you into the cited portion of the page.
