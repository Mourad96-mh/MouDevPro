# Brand Voice — moudevpro.com

> Distilled from the root `CLAUDE.md` (§2, §8, §9). Read this before drafting any page or post.

## Who is speaking

A **freelance web developer** based in Casablanca — one person, not an agency. This is the core
competitive advantage and must come through in every sentence.

## Voice rules

**Always**
- French as primary language. Add EN content on `/marrakech` (tourism) and the intl pages.
- First person singular: **"je crée", "je livre", "contactez-moi"**. Never "nous" / "notre équipe".
- Lead with the outcome: *"Votre site livré en 3 semaines"* — not *"Notre processus de développement"*.
- Mention **"devis gratuit sous 24h"** on every page.
- Professional but personal. Direct, concrete, confident.

**Never**
- "nous" or "notre équipe" — that signals an agency.
- Unverifiable superlatives: "N°1 au Maroc", "meilleur développeur" (this is rhillane's move — don't copy it).
- Generic filler: "solution innovante", "à la pointe de la technologie".
- Copy competitor copy.
- Bury the CTA.

## Differentiator phrases (use naturally)

- "Vous parlez directement avec le développeur"
- "Pas d'intermédiaire, pas d'agence"
- "Qualité agence, prix freelance"
- "Livraison en 7 à 10 jours"
- "Devis gratuit et transparent sous 24h"
- "React, WordPress, WooCommerce — selon vos besoins"
- "Disponible à Casablanca, Rabat et Marrakech"

## CTA standards

| Context | Copy |
| --- | --- |
| Above fold (primary) | Demandez votre devis gratuit |
| Mid-page (secondary) | Obtenir un devis en 24h |
| City pages | Devis gratuit — Casablanca / Rabat / Marrakech |
| Pricing page | Je veux ce pack → |

Never use: "En savoir plus", "Cliquez ici", "Contactez-nous" (too weak). CTA must be visible
without scrolling on mobile (mobile converts best — 3.25% organic CTR vs 0.8% desktop).
