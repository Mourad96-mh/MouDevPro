## 2. Strategic principles for this roadmap

1. **Win on intent, not volume.** Don't chase helloworld's 200 trend posts. Target high-buyer-intent pages (price, devis, city+service).
2. **Pricing transparency is the real moat.** No organic rival publishes structured prices on a dedicated `/tarifs`-style page — the "prix" SERP is all blog guides. We already have the page; make it the best-converting one. Every cluster reinforces it.
3. **"Freelance" is NOT uncontested — sharpen the differentiator.** Half the organic SERP is fellow freelancers (soufianemouahhidi, hicham.tech, ghalimejber, hamza-sehouli, saad-elmoumni, heddoun). "Freelance" alone no longer differentiates. Compete on the concrete trio: **transparent fixed pricing + 7–10 day delivery + direct developer contact**. The agency-vs-freelance angle stays useful only against rhillane / developpeur-informatique (the agencies that actually rank).
4. **Arabic is the real language moat — English is contested.** rhillane runs a full `/en/` section (web-design-agency, custom-website-development, seo-expert-morocco), so EN is a fight, not open ground. **No** organic rival has structured **Arabic** city/price pages — that's the uncontested lane. Prioritise AR; treat EN as competitive.
5. **Stay the specialist.** Resist sprawl to Tanger/Agadir/Fès. Go deep on Casablanca / Rabat / Marrakech — where rhillane and developpeur-informatique already have city pages to out-build.

## 4. 6-month execution plan

> **Re-sequenced 2026-06-03 from Google Ads Search Terms data** (296 clicks, 52 conversions, ~46 MAD CPA, all-time). Three evidence-based changes: (1) e-commerce moved Month 3 → Month 2 (best-converting theme); (2) verticals V2/V5/V6 confirmed by real conversions; (3) city + price intent under-converts in Ads, so organic carries it from Month 1. See `ads-data/negative-keywords.md` for the budget-leak fix.

### Month 1 — Quick wins + pricing moat

- Q1 `/en/casablanca` + `/ar/casablanca`
- Q2 `/tarifs` per-city blocks + FAQ schema
- Q3 "Prix site web Casablanca 2026" post
- Q5 technical/index audit
- **Goal:** close the Casablanca QS gap (CLAUDE.md §5.4) and lock the pricing advantage. **Data justification:** city keywords (`creation site web casablanca`, `agence web rabat`, `agence web marrakech`) draw ad impressions but **0 clicks / 0 conversions** — organic must carry city + price intent because the ads don't.

### Month 2 — E-commerce cluster (moved up — best-converting theme in Ads)

- S2 `/ecommerce` city sections (boutique en ligne Casa/Rabat/Marrakech)
- Pillar A: "Combien coûte un site e-commerce au Maroc" + "Comment créer une boutique en ligne au Maroc"
- M3 EN `/ecommerce`
- **Goal:** capture the e-commerce/boutique-en-ligne intent that **already converts best in our Ads** (~9 conversions from `comment créer une boutique en ligne`, `creer un site e-commerce`, etc.) and stop renting that traffic.

### Month 3 — Freelance positioning

- Q4 "Freelance vs agence" cornerstone post
- Pillar B posts #2–3 (choose-a-dev refresh, WordPress vs Shopify vs React)
- S1 dedicated `/refonte` page
- **Goal:** out-rank fellow freelancers on decision-stage intent by leading with fixed pricing + 7–10 day delivery; use the agency-vs-freelance angle against rhillane / developpeur-informatique.

### Month 4 — Marrakech / tourism + first verticals

- S3 expand `/marrakech` EN booking section
- Pillar C: riad booking post (FR) + hotel website Morocco (EN)
- **V1** `/site-web-hotel-riad` + **V2** `/site-web-restaurant` (tourism verticals, ride the Marrakech momentum)
- **Goal:** own hotel/riad/restaurant developer angle; open the vertical-SEO front rhillane monetises and no freelancer contests.

### Month 5 — Content depth + Arabic moat

- Pillar A: délais + cahier des charges posts
- M2 Arabic `/tarifs` + `/ecommerce`
- Pillar C: site multilingue hôtel/riad post
- **Goal:** topical authority on pricing; uncontested Arabic pages.

### Month 6 — Consolidate, verticals & convert

- S4 `/services` application-web sub-section
- **V3–V5** business verticals (`/site-web-medecin`, `/site-web-avocat`, `/site-web-immobilier`) — only those with a real build/feature list to show
- Pillar B/C remainder
- Internal-linking pass across all clusters → /tarifs + /contact
- Refresh Month-1 posts with new internal links; review Search Console wins
- **Goal:** tighten conversion paths; every cluster + vertical funnels to a devis.
