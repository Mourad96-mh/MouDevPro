# Morocco Web Statistics 2026: 45 Key Figures

Morocco is now one of Africa's most dynamic digital markets. With internet penetration past 92%, 5G launched in late 2025, and e-commerce card payments topping 18 billion dirhams, the numbers speak for themselves. I've gathered 45 verified statistics here — each with its official source — for anyone who wants to understand Morocco's web in 2026: entrepreneurs, online retailers, advertisers, and developers.

## The essentials in 5 figures

- **35.5 million internet users** — 92.2% of Morocco's population online at the end of 2025.
- **22.8 million social media users** — 59.1% of the population.
- **57.1 million mobile connections** — 148% of the population (multiple SIMs per person).
- **18 billion dirhams** in e-commerce card payments in 2025, up 34%.
- **6.8 million active online shoppers** — nearly one in four Moroccan adults.

## 1. Internet users and penetration

**35.5 million internet users** — Morocco's connected population at the end of October 2025. ([DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-morocco))

**92.2%** — internet penetration across the Moroccan population. ([DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-morocco))

**+749,000 users (+2.2%)** — the year-over-year rise in internet users between October 2024 and October 2025. ([DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-morocco))

**38.5 million** — Morocco's total population in October 2025, the base for every penetration rate. ([DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-morocco))

**91.2%** — the share of Moroccans using the internet per the national ICT survey, up from just 74.4% in 2019. ([ANRT, ICT Survey 2024-2025](https://www.anrt.ma/sites/default/files/2025-10/Synth%C3%A8se-Brochure_Fr.pdf))

**93.7% urban vs. 78.4% rural** — the geographic digital divide persists, though rural areas have climbed sharply (66.3% in 2019). ([ANRT, ICT Survey 2024-2025](https://www.anrt.ma/sites/default/files/2025-10/Synth%C3%A8se-Brochure_Fr.pdf))

> **Methodology note:** DataReportal (35.5M) measures connected identities; ANRT/HCP (31.5M) measures individuals declared in a household survey. Both figures are correct — they simply count different things.

**41.18 million internet subscriptions** (fixed + mobile) recorded in Q2 2025, growing 5.21% year over year. ([ANRT, Q2 2025 Observatory](https://lematin.ma/economie/maroc-399-millions-dabonnements-internet-a-fin-mars-2025/285362))

**Over 92% of internet access** in Morocco is via mobile, with 37.02 million subscriptions. ([ANRT, 2025 Observatory](https://lematin.ma/economie/maroc-399-millions-dabonnements-internet-a-fin-mars-2025/285362))

## 2. Mobile and smartphones

**57.1 million active mobile connections** at the end of 2025 — 148% of the population. ([DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-morocco))

**58.75 million mobile subscribers** in Q2 2025 per ANRT, a penetration rate of 159.53%. ([ANRT, Q2 2025 Observatory](https://aujourdhui.ma/societe/indicateurs-des-telecoms-au-2eme-trimestre-2025-le-parc-de-la-telephonie-mobile-a-atteint-587-millions-dabonnes))

**87.7% of mobile connections** are broadband (3G, 4G, or 5G). ([DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-morocco))

**91.7%** — of every 10 people with a mobile phone, more than 9 own a smartphone. ([ANRT, ICT Survey 2024-2025](https://www.anrt.ma/sites/default/files/2025-10/Synth%C3%A8se-Brochure_Fr.pdf))

**32.5 million smartphones** in circulation for 30.7 million active users. ([ANRT, ICT Survey 2024-2025](https://www.anrt.ma/sites/default/files/2025-10/Synth%C3%A8se-Brochure_Fr.pdf))

**90.1% of women** own a smartphone in 2025, up from 73.7% in 2019 — one of the fastest catch-ups. ([ANRT, ICT Survey 2024-2025](https://www.anrt.ma/sites/default/files/2025-10/Synth%C3%A8se-Brochure_Fr.pdf))

**74% of households** own a computer or tablet, up from 60.4% in 2019 (84.4% urban, 49.1% rural). ([ANRT, ICT Survey 2024-2025](https://www.anrt.ma/sites/default/files/2025-10/Synth%C3%A8se-Brochure_Fr.pdf))

*For an online retailer, the takeaway is obvious: your store must be mobile-first. If you're planning an [e-commerce website in Morocco](/ecommerce), mobile isn't optional — it's the default.*

## 3. Social media

**22.8 million social media users** in October 2025 — 59.1% of the population (+1.7 million in a year). ([DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-morocco))

**21.6 million YouTube viewers** in Morocco — more than half the population. ([Morocco World News / DataReportal, 2026](https://www.moroccoworldnews.com/2025/11/267078/morocco-2026-digital-report-92-of-the-population-now-online/))

**16.7 million TikTok users**, up 19% in a single year. ([Morocco World News / DataReportal, 2026](https://www.moroccoworldnews.com/2025/11/267078/morocco-2026-digital-report-92-of-the-population-now-online/))

**15.1 million Instagram users**, up 20% over the year. ([Morocco World News / DataReportal, 2026](https://www.moroccoworldnews.com/2025/11/267078/morocco-2026-digital-report-92-of-the-population-now-online/))

**7.6 million Snapchat users**, up 10%, with a female majority. ([Morocco World News / DataReportal, 2026](https://www.moroccoworldnews.com/2025/11/267078/morocco-2026-digital-report-92-of-the-population-now-online/))

**6.9 million LinkedIn members**, after adding 1.2 million over the year. ([Morocco World News / DataReportal, 2026](https://www.moroccoworldnews.com/2025/11/267078/morocco-2026-digital-report-92-of-the-population-now-online/))

## 4. E-commerce and online payment

**18 billion dirhams** in e-commerce card payments in 2025, up 34% year over year. ([CMI E-commerce Observatory, 2025](https://wafir.ma/en/etudes/observatoire-ecommerce-maroc-2020-2026-bilan-6-ans-cmi-paiements))

**6.8 million active online shoppers** in 2025, up from 1.8 million in 2020 — 278% growth in five years. ([CMI E-commerce Observatory, 2025](https://wafir.ma/en/etudes/observatoire-ecommerce-maroc-2020-2026-bilan-6-ans-cmi-paiements))

**24.9% of Moroccans** bought online in 2024, up from 15.1% in 2019 — +65% and 3.7 million new shoppers. ([ANRT ICT Survey / le360, 2025](https://fr.le360.ma/economie/un-quart-des-marocains-achetent-desormais-en-ligne-en-hausse-de-65-en-cinq-ans_NJBP7FBCGJF4TCG4MKYEVOJMU4/))

**380 dirhams** — the average e-commerce basket in 2025. ([CMI E-commerce Observatory, 2025](https://wafir.ma/en/etudes/observatoire-ecommerce-maroc-2020-2026-bilan-6-ans-cmi-paiements))

**73% of e-commerce transactions** are made from a smartphone. ([CMI E-commerce Observatory, 2025](https://wafir.ma/en/etudes/observatoire-ecommerce-maroc-2020-2026-bilan-6-ans-cmi-paiements))

**47% by card, 38% cash on delivery, 12% M-Wallet** — the split of e-commerce payment methods in 2025. ([CMI E-commerce Observatory, 2025](https://wafir.ma/en/etudes/observatoire-ecommerce-maroc-2020-2026-bilan-6-ans-cmi-paiements))

**84.1% of shoppers** still say they use cash on delivery per the ANRT survey — trust in online payment remains a work in progress. ([ANRT ICT Survey / le360, 2025](https://fr.le360.ma/economie/un-quart-des-marocains-achetent-desormais-en-ligne-en-hausse-de-65-en-cinq-ans_NJBP7FBCGJF4TCG4MKYEVOJMU4/))

**Jumia 28%, Marjane 18%, Glovo 12%** — the leading e-commerce platforms by market share in 2025. ([CMI E-commerce Observatory, 2025](https://wafir.ma/en/etudes/observatoire-ecommerce-maroc-2020-2026-bilan-6-ans-cmi-paiements))

## 5. Connection speed and 5G

**5G launched in November 2025** in Morocco, lifting the country to 39th worldwide for mobile (a 22-place jump). ([Morocco World News / Ookla, 2026](https://www.moroccoworldnews.com/2025/07/232518/morocco-ranks-96th-globally-in-fixed-internet-speed/))

**124.32 Mbps** — the median mobile download speed, the best on the African continent. ([Morocco World News / Ookla, 2026](https://www.moroccoworldnews.com/2025/07/232518/morocco-ranks-96th-globally-in-fixed-internet-speed/))

**60.31 Mbps** — the median mobile download speed measured by DataReportal at end 2025, up 47.5% in a year. ([DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-morocco))

**55.89 Mbps** — the median fixed-connection download speed at end 2025. ([DataReportal, 2026](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-morocco))

## FAQ — Morocco Web Statistics 2026

**How many internet users are there in Morocco in 2026?**
Morocco has 35.5 million internet users at the end of 2025, a 92.2% penetration rate (DataReportal). The ANRT household survey reports 31.5 million individuals, the gap being down to measurement method.

**What is mobile's share of internet access in Morocco?**
Over 92% of internet access is via mobile, with 57.1 million active mobile connections and 87.7% of them on broadband (3G/4G/5G).

**How big is e-commerce in Morocco?**
E-commerce card payments reached 18 billion dirhams in 2025 (+34%), driven by 6.8 million online shoppers. Nearly one in four Moroccans has bought online, and 73% of transactions happen on a smartphone.

**Does Morocco have 5G?**
Yes. 5G launched in November 2025. Morocco has the fastest mobile speed in Africa (124.32 Mbps median) and ranks 39th worldwide.
