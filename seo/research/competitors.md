## The key distinction: paid-only vs organic rivals

The four "agencies" in `../CLAUDE.md` §3 — **helloworld-agency.com, billiontech.ma, mediazain.com,
fes-marketing.net** — **do not rank organically** for the core money keywords. They compete on
**paid ads only**. Useful for ad-copy and content ideas, but they are _not_ the organic battlefield.
Don't shape organic pages around them.

The real organic competition is below.

## Positioning rules that fall out of this (use in every draft)

1. Lead with the **concrete trio**, not the word "freelance": transparent fixed price, 7–10 day
   delivery, direct developer contact.
2. **Surface price in `<title>` / H1** on money pages — beat creationsiteweb's CTR trick with real numbers.
3. **Make `/tarifs` the moat** — the only structured price page in a SERP full of vague blog guides.
4. **Arabic is open ground**; English is contested (rhillane runs a full `/en/`). Prioritise AR pages.
5. **Stay the specialist** — Casablanca / Rabat / Marrakech only. Out-build rhillane and
   developpeur-informatique on their own city pages; don't sprawl to Tanger/Agadir/Fès.
6. Only ship vertical/niche pages you can back with a real build or concrete feature list — never
   thin doorway pages (that's rhillane's volume game, not yours).
